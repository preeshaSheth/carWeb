About Us

At ISKCAR, we believe in "Seedhi Baat, No Bakwass." Since our inception in 2000, our name, derived from ISK - ISKCON (Temple) and CAR - car dealing, reflects our straightforward approach and dedication to excellence. As direct sales associates, we specialize in the buying and selling of new and used cars of all brands. Our commitment is to provide our clients with the best deals and unparalleled service, ensuring a seamless and satisfying experience every time.
